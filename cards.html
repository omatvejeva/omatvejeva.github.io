Material Exploration: Plastic Wrap

For this project, the focus is on material exploration and the materiality of a chosen material. We experimented and documented the changes of our chosen material as it comes into contact with outside stimuli or is put under pressure. Alongside the experimentation, we conducted research to better understand the material we were working with. After conducting the research and experimentation, we created a handheld prototype that showed off the materiality of the material.

Community Garden

This project was an exploration of reclaiming urban spaces with community gardens. We researched an urban site that would benefit from being used as a community garden and conducted research on the social, environmental, and urban planning of said proposed community gardens. Also, I created a digital floor plan for my proposed garden with a focus on accessibility and sustainability of the design.
