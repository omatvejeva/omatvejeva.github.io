Hostile Architecture

This project was an exploration of the hostile architecture we encounter on a daily basis, and how it discourages certain acts or behaviors in public areas. We were challenged to analyze hostile architecture and its exclusive intents and create a human-centred, innovative design to aid and reclaim public spaces for the community’s well-being. We were challenged to create a prototype for the intended space and show how it would aid the community.
